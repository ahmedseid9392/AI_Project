What This Project Does

A single platform for student data, prediction workflows, and intervention planning.

Centralize Student Records

Maintain registered student profiles with academic and behavioral indicators in one place for easier review and monitoring.

Generate Predictions

Use trained machine learning models to estimate likely grades, scores, and confidence levels from current student inputs.

Support Better Decisions

Surface recommendations that help teachers and students focus on attendance, study behavior, and other meaningful performance drivers.

How It Works

1
Register Users

Create accounts for students, teachers, and administrators with role-based access.

2
Upload Training Data

Admins upload CSV datasets, choose the target column, and train the prediction model.

3
Predict Performance

Students or staff enter current learning indicators and receive predicted outcomes.

4
Review History

Keep a record of predictions to compare results, confidence, and follow-up actions.

Who It Helps

Administrators

Manage users, upload datasets, monitor dashboard summaries, and oversee model readiness.

Teachers

Review registered students, create predictions, and identify learners who may need support.

Students

Check personal prediction history, understand performance trends, and act on recommendations for improvement.
